The Training and Placement Office at PDA College of Engineering provides guidance on all aspects of career planning, job searching and post-graduate studies. The Cell strives to provide individual counseling for all the students towards their career goals. The College has an independent Training and Placement Office headed by a Professor designated as Training and Placement Officer and supported by a Placement Coordinator of each department.

The Training and Placement Cell has been striving continuously for the placement of the students in reputed organizations. Also, assistance is given to students who are not placed through campus interviews, to take up jobs through off campus mode. Reputed national and multinational companies visit the college for campus recruitment annually. Good number of the students secure job offers before they complete their courses.

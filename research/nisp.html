Vision and Mission
Vision

To create a sustainable research and innovation ecosystem in the institute for creation of successful entrepreneurs.

Mission
  • Creating entrepreneurship awareness amongst the stake holders.
  • Supporting applied research and innovation through well developed research infrastructure.
  • Promoting the stake holders in setting up technology driven enterprise.
Short Term Objectives
  • To Promote Innovation, Creative and Design Thinking.
  • To Organize Seminar, Workshops and Training Programmes for building entrepreneurial culture and to spread awareness regarding IPR related activities.
  • To Support students to prototype their ideas.
  • To facilitate incubation for faculty/staff driven start-up and student/Alumni start-up.
  • To Enhance the Institute-Industry Interaction Cell activities, by associating with DST, CII, MSME and other academic institutions.
  • To establish strategic partnership linkage with
    1. Entrepreneurship Development Institute of India (EDII).
    2. National Innovation Foundation (NIF) of India to submit ideas and apply for schemes.
    3. Procure fund from AICTE for Entrepreneurship Development Cell.
Long Term Objectives
  • To establish Technology /Business Incubators / Accelerators in each of the different sectors.
  • To facilitate Venture Capital funding.
  • To encourage/facilitate/incubate at least 10 innovative Technology Solutions in sectors like Health Care, Agriculture, Clean Environment, Advanced Computing and Sustainable Materials.
  • Commencement of Masters/MBA programs in Entrepreneurship.
  • To provide a platform for students to develop innovative products with global recognition and generate business opportunities.
  • To improve quality of research work to attain patents which can be commercially used in production.
  • To generate revenues through consultancy work and student start-ups.
